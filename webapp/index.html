Security Manifesto

🚫

Hardened CSP

The connect-src 'none' policy physically prevents the browser from making any network requests. Data exfiltration is impossible.

🧠

WASM Engine

Parsing is performed by a high-performance Rust engine compiled to WebAssembly, running in a sandboxed Web Worker.

🌪️

Zero Persistence

Transactions exist only in volatile RAM. No data is written to localStorage or IndexedDB. A refresh wipes everything.

About VaultHDFC

Private Credit Card Analysis

VaultHDFC was built for people who want to understand their spending without uploading sensitive bank statements to third-party servers.

Every calculation, from PDF decryption to category matching, happens entirely on your machine.

Key Features

  • Multi-file support: Upload several months at once.
  • Fast: Rust-powered parsing takes milliseconds.
  • Offline: Works without an internet connection.
