Analysis Settings

Configure how transactions are categorized based on their description.

How Categorization Works

  • Partial Match: The analyzer checks if the transaction description contains any of your keywords (case-insensitive).
  • Comma Separated: Enter multiple keywords separated by commas (e.g. Amazon, Amzn, AWS).
  • Priority: The analyzer checks categories from top to bottom. The first match wins.

How it works

Understanding the privacy-first architecture of this utility.

Privacy by Design

Financial data is deeply sensitive. Traditional spend analyzers require you to upload your bank statements to their servers, creating a significant privacy risk. This tool is designed to respect your data by ensuring it never leaves your device.

All decryption, parsing, and analysis are performed by a high-performance Rust engine compiled to WebAssembly (WASM), running entirely within your browser's local memory.

Technical Transparency

Security Safeguards

  • Zero Networking: A strict browser policy blocks all external network requests.
  • In-Memory Only: Statements are processed in volatile RAM and are lost immediately on page refresh.
  • No Tracking: There are no analytics, cookies, or tracking scripts included in this tool.

Privacy Verification

This utility is designed for high-sensitivity financial data. Your browser enforces the following local-only environment:

  • Network Isolation: All outgoing requests are physically blocked by the browser.
  • WASM Engine: PDF parsing happens in a sandboxed binary thread.
  • Volatile Memory: No data is stored in cookies, databases, or local storage.
